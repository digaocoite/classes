Evaluate: BSCS Five Es Strategy Worksheet

Phase Instructor Responsibilities Learning Strategies to Foster
ENGAGEMENT
The instructor uses strategies designed to engage learner interest, determine what learners know about the topic and identify any misconceptions.		 Provide slides based on a Simpsons vacation scenario to engage students and introduce the subjunctive mood (Slides Link).
Play a fun YouTube video clip depicting hypothetical scenarios from the show. 		Students share personal experiences related to vacations or traveling, using hypothetical expressions (e.g., "¿Qué harías si ganaras la lotería?", "¿A dónde viajarías si pudieras elegir cualquier lugar del mundo?").
Initial exposure to subjunctive forms embedded in everyday contexts to spark interest and curiosity.
EXPLORATION
Learners engage in cooperative activities, exploring and creating common experiences to provide a basis for learning concepts and skills.		 Conduct cooperative activities for students to collaboratively explore and discuss hypothetical scenarios from the Simpsons slides. For example, discussing scenarios such as "Si fueras Bart, ¿qué harías en la playa?" Cooperative learning and peer interaction.
Identifying language patterns from context.
EXPLANATION
Learners use their own words to explain their activities, and to listen critically to one another, clarifying and correcting explanations along with the instructor. Links are made between learner explanations and their previous experiences. Instructor introduces more formal explanations and terminology.		 Clearly explain the subjunctive mood, including grammatical structures, usage rules, and common expressions, using examples like "Es importante que vengas temprano." Understanding formal grammar rules through direct instruction.
ELABORATION
Learners apply the new concepts and skills in a new situation to build deeper understanding by extending and elaborating on the new knowledge.		 Conduct a Kahoot quiz to reinforce understanding and recognition of subjunctive forms.
Engage students in a live Quizlet session (Quizlet link).
Analyze the song "A Dios le Pido" by Juanes in small groups (Activity PDF), highlighting phrases like "Que mis ojos se despierten con la luz de tu mirada." 		Application of new knowledge to different contexts.
Interactive and collaborative analysis of authentic materials.
EVALUATION
Instructor appraises learner progress toward learning goals by checking understanding through formal and informal assessments and observations. Learners also self-assess.		 Conduct a conversational exercise where students use the subjunctive mood to discuss hypothetical vacation plans (e.g., "Si pudieras viajar mañana, ¿a dónde irías?").
Observe students’ accuracy and fluency in using subjunctive forms. 		Self-assessment through Quizlet live and Kahoots.
Peer and instructor feedback during conversational exercises. Short Quiz on Canvas to make sure students undersatand verb conjugation in the subjunctive.

Compiled from Bybee, 1997; Bybee et al., 2006; and Morgan & Ansberry, 2007.
