Evaluate: BSCS Five Es Strategy Worksheet

Phase Instructor Responsibilities Learning Strategies to Foster
ENGAGEMENT Provide slides based on a Simpsons vacation scenario to engage students and introduce the subjunctive mood (Slides Link).
Play a fun YouTube video clip depicting hypothetical scenarios from the show. 		Students share personal experiences related to vacations, using hypothetical expressions.
Initial exposure to subjunctive forms embedded in everyday contexts to spark interest and curiosity.
EXPLORATION Conduct cooperative activities for students to collaboratively explore and discuss hypothetical scenarios from the Simpsons slides. Cooperative learning and peer interaction.
Identifying language patterns from context.
EXPLANATION Clearly explain the subjunctive mood, including grammatical structures, usage rules, and common expressions. Understanding formal grammar rules through direct instruction.
ELABORATION Conduct a Kahoot quiz to reinforce understanding and recognition of subjunctive forms.
Engage students in a live Quizlet session (Quizlet link).
Analyze the song "A Dios le Pido" by Juanes in small groups (Activity PDF). 		Application of new knowledge to different contexts.
Interactive and collaborative analysis of authentic materials.
EVALUATION Conduct a conversational exercise where students use the subjunctive mood to discuss hypothetical vacation plans.
Observe students’ accuracy and fluency in using subjunctive forms. 		Self-assessment through Quizlet live.
Peer and instructor feedback during conversational exercises.

Compiled from Bybee, 1997; Bybee et al., 2006; and Morgan & Ansberry, 2007.
