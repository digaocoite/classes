Rubric: Personalized Informal Learning Plan

Criteria Excellent (4 pts) Proficient (3 pts) Developing (2 pts) Needs Improvement (1 pt)
Diverse Learning Resources Includes 3+ relevant and clearly explained informal learning resources, each tied to specific goals Includes 3 relevant resources with some explanation of purpose Includes fewer than 3 resources or lacks clarity in how they relate to goals Resources are missing or not relevant to learning goals
Structured Timeline Timeline is clear, realistic, and includes goals, time commitments, and checkpoints Timeline includes most required elements; may lack some clarity or feasibility Timeline is vague or missing one key element (e.g., goals or checkpoints) Timeline is incomplete, unrealistic, or missing altogether
Personal Relevance Plan strongly reflects personal learning goals and context; demonstrates thoughtful reflection Plan shows some connection to personal goals; some reflection evident Plan makes a basic connection to goals but lacks depth Plan appears generic or disconnected from learner’s context
Integration of Course Concepts Effectively integrates insights from module videos, discussion, and activity Uses some ideas from the module to support the plan Briefly references module content without much depth No clear connection to module content or experiences
Organization and Presentation Well-organized, easy to follow; uses headings/visuals appropriately (if applicable) Organized with clear sections; mostly easy to follow Some issues with structure or clarity Disorganized, hard to follow, lacks clear structure

Total Possible Points: 20

Suggested Grading Scale:

  • 18–20 = Excellent
  • 15–17 = Proficient
  • 12–14 = Developing
  • Below 12 = Needs Improvement
